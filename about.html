Extensive Experience Over the Years

Life Enrichment Institute is a premier training and consultancy firm based in Ngong, Kajiado County, Kenya. Registered under certificate no. BN/2013/208052, we are also accredited by the National Industrial Training Authority (NITA) with registration number NITA/TRN/2518 to offer training in Emotional Intelligence, Strategic Management, and Corporate Governance.

At Life Enrichment Institute, we believe that every individual possesses a seed of greatness, waiting to be nurtured and developed. Our mission is to equip individuals and organizations with the skills necessary for peak performance and success. Through our learner-centered approach and experiential learning techniques, we work with qualified, passionate, and dedicated facilitators to ensure our programs inspire transformation and personal growth.

We specialize in delivering cutting-edge training solutions that help both individuals and organizations achieve excellence. Our goal is to provide practical skills that enhance not only personal development but also organizational effectiveness. Whether you are a manager looking to develop leadership skills or an individual striving for self-improvement, Life Enrichment Institute is your trusted partner in success.
